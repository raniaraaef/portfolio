## 🌟 EASIEST METHOD: Netlify Drop (No Account Needed First!)

### Steps (Takes 2 minutes):
1. Go to: https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag and drop your ENTIRE portfolio folder onto the page
3. Wait 30 seconds
4. You'll get a live link instantly!
5. (Optional) Sign up to claim your site and customize the URL

**That's it! Your portfolio will be live!**

## 🔥 METHOD 2: Vercel (Professional, Same as the link you sent)

### Steps:
1. **Create GitHub Account** (if you don't have one)
   - Go to: https://github.com/signup
   - Sign up with your email

2. **Create a New Repository**
   - Click the "+" icon → "New repository"
   - Name it: `portfolio`
   - Make it Public
   - Click "Create repository"

3. **Upload Your Files**
   - Click "uploading an existing file"
   - Drag ALL your portfolio files:
     * index.html
     * styles.css
     * script.js
     * README.md
     * store.png
     * clinic.png
     * sknny.jpg
     * photo_1_2026-02-26_18-59-21.jpg
   - Click "Commit changes"

4. **Deploy to Vercel**
   - Go to: https://vercel.com/signup
   - Click "Continue with GitHub"
   - Click "Import Project"
   - Select your `portfolio` repository
   - Click "Deploy"
   - Wait 1 minute
   - **Done!** You'll get a URL like: `portfolio-raniaraaef.vercel.app`

5. **Customize Your URL** (Optional)
   - Go to Project Settings → Domains
   - Add custom domain like: `raniaraaef.vercel.app`

## 📱 METHOD 3: GitHub Pages (Free Forever)

### Steps:
1. **Create GitHub Account**
   - Go to: https://github.com/signup

2. **Create Special Repository**
   - Click "+" → "New repository"
   - Name it EXACTLY: `raniaraaef.github.io`
   - Make it Public
   - Click "Create repository"

3. **Upload Files**
   - Click "uploading an existing file"
   - Upload ALL your portfolio files
   - Click "Commit changes"

4. **Enable GitHub Pages**
   - Go to Settings → Pages
   - Source: Deploy from a branch
   - Branch: main
   - Click Save

5. **Your Portfolio is Live!**
   - URL: `https://raniaraaef.github.io`
   - Wait 2-3 minutes for it to go live
